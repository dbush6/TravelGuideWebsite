1. Eligibility & ID

Confirm first-time applicant. Gather proof of citizenship + photo ID.

2. Photo

Recent color photo, correct size, neutral background, no glasses.

3. DS-11

Fill online to print or handwrite in black ink. Do not sign yet.

4. Appointment

Find an acceptance facility (post office/court) and schedule.

5. Fees

Application + execution; consider expedited processing if needed.

6. Submit

Bring originals + photocopies. Sign in presence of the agent.

7. Track & Receive

Track status; add mailing time on top of processing.

Common Mistakes ✅

  • Using DS-82 instead of DS-11
  • Wrong photo size/background
  • Signing before meeting the agent
  • Submitting copies instead of originals
  • Booking travel before passport arrives
